Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about vehicle data monetization

What if I don't like the project I chose?

Most projects offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. We also provide detailed reviews and comparison tools to help you choose the right project from the start.

How does this compare to traditional vehicle data collection?

Unlike traditional data collection where companies profit from your data without compensation, blockchain-based projects give you ownership and control. You decide what to share and receive fair compensation through tokens.

Is it hard to set up and use?

Most projects can be set up within 2-5 minutes. App-based solutions require just downloading an app, while device-based ones use simple plug-and-play hardware that connects to your car's OBD port.

How much can I actually earn?

Earnings vary based on how much you drive, your location, and which project(s) you choose. Most users report earning between $30-150 per month. Use our calculator to get a personalized estimate.

How do I know these projects are secure?

All projects listed on Drive2Earn.io undergo a thorough security audit by our blockchain experts. We verify smart contract security, data privacy practices, and review the team's credentials before listing any project.
